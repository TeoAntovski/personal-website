HOBBIES

Chess

One of my favorite hobbies is playing chess on my PC. I have been playing for about three years, and over time I’ve really improved my skills. I enjoy the challenge of thinking ahead and strategizing to outsmart my opponent. Chess helps me develop patience and focus, and it’s always exciting to learn new tactics and openings.

Watching Series and Movies

I also love watching series and movies in my free time. Some of my all-time favorite shows are Breaking Bad, Better Call Saul, and The Walking Dead. I find these series really captivating because of their intense stories and well-developed characters. I’m especially drawn to science fiction, as I enjoy exploring imaginative worlds and futuristic ideas, which sets it apart from other genres I watch.

Protecting Skopje

In the evenings, I patrol the streets of Skopje as Batman, protecting the city from all evil forces. It’s rewarding work keeping the citizens safe, though it can be exhausting balancing my vigilante duties with my other hobbies. This responsibility adds real purpose and excitement to my everyday life.
